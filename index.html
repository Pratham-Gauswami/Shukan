About Us

Welcome to BooBurr Boutique

Welcome to BooBurr Boutique, your go-to place for thoughtful and creative gift baskets. Our website helps you find the perfect gifts for friends, family, and other special people in your life. BooBurr Boutique allows you to explore our work, track the progress of your orders, and get inspiration for what to include in your custom baskets. We specialize in seasonal gifting for occasions such as Halloween and Christmas, as well as celebrations like birthdays and anniversaries.

About Me

A little introduction about me: my name is Shukan Goswami, and I started BooBurr Boutique in July during the summer. I have been running this business for almost seven months now, and I currently manage everything on my own. Throughout this time, I have created and delivered many Boo and Burr baskets, gaining valuable experience along the way. Many customers have even come to me outside of holiday seasons to create special gifts for their friends and loved ones, which motivates me to continue growing my business.
